What is subtraction of integers?

To successfully perform the task of subtracing numbers, you must know what subtraction is. The term "subtraction" represents a mathematical operation. Subtraction is a process, an action that you do perform using numbers. Subtraction allows you find the difference of 2 numbers.

You use the minu ( - ) button to express the subtraction operation in the app.

In the subtraction process, you take a number and reduce it to a smaller number. That is, we take away one number from another.
